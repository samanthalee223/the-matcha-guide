Why Make Matcha at Home?

With the rising global popularity of matcha, it's becoming easier and easier to find matcha at almost every cafe. However, the quality of the matcha is often poor for a high price.

Making matcha at home is both cost effective and can be catered to personal preference, allowing for genuine appreciation for matcha's complex flavours. It also means less single-use plastic, which ensures the tradition of matcha is sustainable and long lasting.
